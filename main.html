Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. It is famous for its art, culture, architecture, fashion, and delicious food. The city attracts millions of tourists every year with its iconic landmarks and romantic atmosphere.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most famous landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A historic and stunning cathedral.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A popular avenue known for shopping and cafes.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A relaxing way to explore the city.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A buttery and flaky French pastry.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicious sweet treats.
  • French Onion Soup – A classic French dish.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes served with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Baguette – Traditional French bread loved worldwide.
