Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. It is famous for its art, culture, fashion, and delicious cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Famous for the Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A historic church with stunning architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A popular avenue for shopping and dining.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A relaxing way to explore the city.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A buttery French pastry.
  • Macarons – Sweet colorful cookies.
  • French Onion Soup – A traditional French dish.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with tasty fillings.
  • Baguette – Famous French bread.
